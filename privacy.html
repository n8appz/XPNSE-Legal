Privacy Policy

XPNSE · Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the XPNSE app (“XPNSE,” “we,” “our,” or “the app”) handles your information. XPNSE is a personal budgeting app designed with privacy as a core principle.

Data We Collect

XPNSE does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal or financial information. All financial data you enter — including transactions, accounts, budgets, categories, and goals — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that receive or store your financial data, and we do not have access to it.

No Bank Connections

XPNSE does not connect to your bank, does not ask for banking credentials, and does not use third-party financial aggregation services. All data entry is manual. Because we never receive your banking login information, that information cannot be stored, shared, or exposed through XPNSE.

Subscriptions and Payments

XPNSE offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions for premium features. All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Apple provides us with limited, aggregated information about subscription status (for example, whether a subscription is active) so the app can unlock premium features. Apple’s handling of your payment information is governed by Apple’s own privacy policy.

Device Permissions

XPNSE may request access to certain device features, such as Face ID, solely to provide app functionality (for example, locking the app for your privacy). Authentication is handled by your device’s operating system; XPNSE does not receive or store your biometric data.

Analytics and Tracking

XPNSE does not include third-party advertising or cross-app tracking. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Data Backup and Export

XPNSE allows you to export your data for your own backup purposes. Any exported files are created at your request and saved to a location you choose. You are responsible for the security of files you export and store outside the app.

Children’s Privacy

XPNSE is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@victusapp.com.
